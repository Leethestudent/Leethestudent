Admissions

Thank you for your interest in Jane Academy. These are the admission requirements for your child to be considered at our institution:

  1. A copy of the birth certificate
  2. A copy the parents/guardians ID card/Passport
  3. School report from the student's latest school (if applicable)
  4. Student and/or Parent/Guardian entrance interview
  5. Completion of online application
