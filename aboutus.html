About Us


Our Story

Our school offers the best of the Kenyan Cirruculum(8-4-4) coupled with the British system.

We aim to be one of the foremost educationational institutions in the country.


Vision

We plan to be a cutting edge school that blend the best learning environment, with great facilities to equip our students with a great outlook for the future.

Our school also offers the best co-curricular activities aiming at helping our students become well rounded and well balanced individuals in all aspects of life.
